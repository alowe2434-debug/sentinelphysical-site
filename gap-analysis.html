Scope of Deliverables
What the Gap Analysis delivers
Four structured documents. No raw notes, no generic summaries. Everything is formatted for direct use in a TJC pre-survey review or a board-level facilities briefing.
01
PE Chapter Crosswalk Map
Every EC and LS standard reference in your existing documentation mapped line-by-line to the corresponding 2026 PE element. Shows exactly where old language maps to new requirements — and where it doesn't.
02
Red / Yellow / Green Gap Report
Structured findings across every PE.04.01.01 element. Red = active deficiency risk. Yellow = documentation gap requiring remediation before next survey. Green = compliant and documented. Actionable, not academic.
03
Maintenance Management Program Review
Assessment of your MMP documentation against the 2026 ITM (Inspection, Testing, and Maintenance) requirements under PE.04.01.01. Identifies whether your inspection frequency, documentation format, and contractor credentials meet the current standard.
04
30-Minute Debrief Call
Live walkthrough of findings with your Life Safety Officer or Director of Facilities. We answer questions, prioritize the remediation list, and explain what a surveyor would flag first. No slides — just direct answers to direct questions.

How It Works
Five steps from booking to delivery
The entire engagement is remote. No site visit required for the Gap Analysis. You share documentation; we do the analysis.
1
Book a 15-minute intake call
Confirm facility type, accrediting body (TJC or DNV), last survey date, and documentation format. We tell you exactly what to send us.
2
Submit your documentation package
Current Life Safety Logbook, most recent fire and smoke damper inspection reports, MMP documentation, and any prior TJC findings related to the Physical Environment chapter.
3

We review every document against the 2026 PE chapter framework, map all EC/LS references to PE elements, and identify every gap — documented or undocumented.
4

Four structured documents delivered by day 5: Crosswalk Map, Gap Report, MMP Review, and a prioritized remediation list sorted by survey risk.
5

Live call to walk through findings, answer questions, and if needed, scope the full Compliance Map and field inspection engagement. The Gap Analysis fee is credited in full toward any follow-on contract.
